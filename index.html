This website is made to inform you about most (possibly all) of the blood disorders and cancers there is.
Terms:
  • Blood Cells: most abundant cell in you body, made in your bone.
    • Red Blood Cell (RBCs): carries oxygen around your body.
    • White Blood Cells: fight off diseases.
    • Platelets: clogs wounds so you wouldn't bleed out.
    • Hemoglobin: protein that allows red blood cells to carry oxygen.
  • Mitosis: process of cell division.
  • Hereditary: genetic, passed down through genes.
Blood Disorders:

These blood disorders can happen to anyone. Some are inevitable because they are hereditary. Some can be caused by lack of nutrients in your diet.

Blood Cancers:

These blood cancers can happen to anyone. Some are caused by radiation that damage your cells which then causes them to multiply out of control. There may also be mistakes during mitosis that causes the same thing.
